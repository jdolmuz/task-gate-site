Common Questions

Do I need an account?

No. Task Gate does not require an account for this release.

Where is my task data stored?

Your task data is stored locally on your device.

Does Task Gate sync across devices?

No. Cloud sync is not included in this release.

How do I clear old tasks?

Use the clear controls in the Queue and Finished sections of the app.
