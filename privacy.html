Task Gate Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Overview

Task Gate is a local-first productivity app. For this release, your task data is stored locally on your device. Task Gate does not require an account and does not provide cloud sync.

Data Collection

Task Gate does not collect personal data from the app. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or cloud services to collect task content or usage information.

Local App Data

Tasks, queued items, completed items, and related app state are stored on your device so the app can preserve your workflow between launches. This information is not sent to us.

Support Email

If you email support, we will receive the email address and message you send. We use that information only to respond to your support request.

Changes

If Task Gate adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or other data collection in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before those changes are released.
